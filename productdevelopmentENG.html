Product development

Wheter starting from a simple sketch or a complete design we will provide our expertise and experience in manufacturing to assist you with setting up a cost effective manufacturing process.

If you have already completed the designs this will simplify the manufacturing process but if not our expert team are there to help you. We use multiple design programs to design and specify the parts together with you, before moving into production. By working this way we can ensure that all parts are manufactured in the correct way to your specifications.

For information and examples of products we have helped to develop and manufacture click here.

We are able to design and manufacture most metalwork components.

Once the design is approved the manufacturing begins. We have the experience and resources to produce metalwork products with precision and high quality.
