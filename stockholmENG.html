Welcome to Alumbra Stockholm!

Phone: +46 858 085 200
Email: Info@alumbra.se

Our facilities are in Järfälla outside of Stockholm. We focus on smaller series with high complexity, jobs with high quality requirments and problem solving for the customer.

Business plan - Aluminium dip brazing and high tech mechanical construction.

We are one of a few companies in the world that specializes in aluminium dip brazing and high tech manufacturing of mechanical components in aluminium. Our work is demanding, challenging and super fun. Here everything is a possibility. You up limits, we push them. Our motto is curiosity and ingenuity. Precision and specialisation gets mixed with common sense and critical thinking. Quality over quantity.

We like to describe our selfs as unique both in terms of our business and our employess. Each member is hand picked, well educated, experienced and close to irreplaceable. It is this depth of knowledge you take part of when you choose us.

Our clients are mainly in Sweden but we also export to among others. England, Spain, Germany, Poland and Italy. Among our most meaningful clients are Sivers Lab Kista, Note Norrtelje and SAAB, where we work with multiple divisons in Sweden.

Examples of our work
Adapter for countermeasure trap

We have manufactured advanced adapter plates for a countermeasure trap. This was about very high precision and arched surfaces. It was manufactured in the 4-axis MoriSeiki machine with 2,5D CAM.


Wave guide

To rationalize in brazing of wave guides we reconstructed and made the wave guide details in halves, that we later join together by dip brazing. This gives a significant simplification compared to the traditional method, that uses multiple pipes, bends and flanges(???)

SAAB - Gripen

We were part of the development and manufactured several vital components in the JAG Gripen such as antennas, heat exchangers and weapon systems.
