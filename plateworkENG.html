METAL WORKING

Alumbra Urshult does most thing metal working and has modern equipment such as a punch press, press break, eccentric press and weld. Our strength is delivering advanced metal working in a cost effective manner.

Our depth of knowledge and experience working with metal means we can handle most types of sheet metal.
