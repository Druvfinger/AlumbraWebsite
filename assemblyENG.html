Assembly

We offer our customers a full range of solutions from production to reliable delivery to customers. This means we often are contracted to do the metalworking as well as the assembly and function control.

We have 2000 ㎡ assembly floor and often complete final assembly for our customers. Final assembly has the benefit that we can store the products in our 1000 ㎡ warehouse to later deliver directly to the end customer. We have knowledgeable and experienced fitters that can assemble most products.

Warehousing

In our warehouse we can in a space efficient way handle our customers' products. The warehouse gives us flexibility, shorter lead times and a customer ordered controlled production.

Your products can be stored with us. Manufactured and finished on the shelves waiting for delivery to the end customer.

Powder coating

This facility is made up of a combined 2‐step iron phosphating with wash, drying‐kiln and 3 powder application boxes.
(2 with friction charges and 1 with electrostatic charge) as well as a kiln. Mostly white mix powder is applied.
On order all powder types can be run (epoxy, mix, polyester and metallic) and colors (NCS‐ and RAL‐scale)

Max product size: L=210cm, W=65cm, H=140cm

The entire washing facility is run with recirculation, and used baths are run through vacuum evaporators, where none of the processed water is released.
