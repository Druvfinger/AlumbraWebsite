MECHANICAL WORKSHOP

A large part of our business happens by our CNC machines. Here it's about advanced CAM‐systems that read all types of CAD files. On site are also our capable operators. Good people are hard to come by and impossible to replace. As a customer you can turn to us with your questions or wishes. It can be early on in the process or late in your own process. You don't need fully developed designs. We quickly manufacture prototypes within precision mechanics. We can adjust, modify or repair products, partially in the development cycle of a new product or for products already in the market.

We have a plethora of conventional processing machines where multiple of them are high precision. We also have eight CNC‐controlled milling machines and four CNC‐controlled lathes, two with powered tools. Most file formats and solids can be handled in the CAM‐software. Short lead times, product quality and your high requirements are a fun challenge for us that need to be met.
