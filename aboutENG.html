Welcome to Alumbra

We are a metalworking company that provides contract manufacturing in our production facilities in Sweden.

We offer a full range of metalworking technologies, for example aluminium brazing,
plate processing, surface treatment and CNC Milling.

We deliver everything from singular components to fully assembled solutions to customers throughout Europe and the US.

Vision & business idea

Our goal is to take on the processes needed to satisfy the client request, starting from developing and purchasing all the way to assembling the product and delivering it to the customer.

This way we can ensure the quality of the product as well as decreasing the risk of misunderstandings and delays. This is because we know the product well, along with the entire creation process of it.

When you outsource production to us at Alumbra it should result in higher flexibility, a cost efficient process and reliable delivery. We are here to make your work easier and with our innovative spirit we make ourselves the obvious choice!

History

After world war II Sweden built a strong production industry. Sweden was growing and with companies like Ericsson, Esselte, TK Industries, Electrolux to name a few we were a giant in the production industry. These industries employed tens of thousands of people. Sweden had world leading competence in production.

With time other industries were prioritized among them product development and research. These were said to create more value for Sweden. Many other countries saw the importance of a strong domestic production industry, among these were China, Poland and large parts of the Baltic countries. These countries chose to invest and build up this industry.

A consequence of this was that during the 90s Swedish production started to move abroad. Today only a fraction of the once strong Swedish production industry remains. The sad part about this is not only that the jobs left but so did a large part of the competence we had spent the last few decades procuring.

We founded Alumbra Group since we believe it’s important to have a strong domestic production industry. We believe this for multiple reasons. We think it’s important for startups to have access to local production competence. We also believe that in times of crisis that it’s important to have domestic production (For example geopolitical or logistical crises). We also think it’s good for our environment to have local production so we can reduce for example transportation and more.

We think its important that Sweden once again become a strong production country and we full heartedly believe we can be.
Alumbra has the competence , the staff and most importantly the will to rebuild this once thriving industry.

Alumbra Stockholm
1963 Philips established dip brazing in Stockholm. This business was integrated in to SAAB who worked with it during many years. Year 2000 SAAB decides to leave metal produktion so the production manager buys out part of the company and creates Alumbra. 2014 Alumbra aqquires EKAB Finmekanik. Five years later Alumbra Group is created and aquires the Urshult business.

Alumbra Profilteknik
Järfälla Profilteknik was created 1981 by Jorma Lustig with the business plan of quickly and efficently be able to deliver high quality products with the help of modern equipment and knowledgable staff. 2021 Järfälla Profilteknik was aquired by Alumbra Group and became Alumbra Profilteknik.

Alumbra Urshult
The factory in Urshult was built 1958 and was run until 1981 when it was aquired by Esselte. Esslete runs the factory for another 20 years when Hans Nilsson and Bengt Kronholm buys the operation and becomes Agrippa. 13 years after the formation of Agrippa they aquire Alumbra Maskinteknik. and within the next three years they purchase the property. 2019 the business is aquired by ALumbra Group and becomes Alumbra Urshult.

Alumbra Småland
BK Produkter was founded 1985 by Börje Klasson. Together with his son Fredrik they built up a modern machine park with knowledgable workers. 2014 the company was aquired by Sven-Erik Nilsson and Birgitta Bjäde. Within the next four years they take over the produktion on Alvesta 3 Plåt. 2023 Alumbra Group aquires BK Produkter with the plan of merging the company with Alumbra Urshult and create Alumbra Småland.
