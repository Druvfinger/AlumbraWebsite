GDPR

Alumbra acts in accordance to current data protection laws (GDPR). We register and store information in the customer and
supplier registry. We handle sensitive data about you: name, email and phone number.

As a customer at Alumbra your details are only handled when necessary, to administer the customer relationship and to uphold our commitments towards you.

Your data is saved as long as the customer relationship is active and
there after the time necessary or is allowed by laws.
You have the right at any time and without reason to get your data deleted by contacting info@alumbra.se
