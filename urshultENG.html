We are located in the southern part of Småland in the scenic town of Urshult by lake Åsnen.
Our clients are given the freedom to develop products and focus on sales while we manufacture and warehouse their products.
We are your full scale contract manufacturer for everything sheet metal and offer preparation, manufacturing and warehousing.
We specialise in cabinets, panels, plinths and profiles as well as large or small technical service kiosks.

Our Business Model is to Provide Our Customers with Cost Effective Contract Manufacturing

Our vision is to be a contract manufacturer of original metal products to industry, offices and retail within Europe.
Our business is based around 9001/14001-certification. Our philosophy is production efficiency, product quality and customer service.

Service/Contract Manufacturing ‐ From Idea to Finished Product

We provide our extensive manufacturing and production knowledge. A full range solution with construction, production, assembly, warehousing and delivery all in one place.
This allows us to give you short lead times and the opportunity to create high quality products.
